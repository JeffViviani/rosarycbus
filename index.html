WELCOME

Welcome to Rosary Cbus! We are a group that prays the rosary every night together. This website serves as a resource for the group, but it is also a free resource for anybody on the web looking how to pray the rosary.

While the same basic structure exists across rosaries, one will find that different sources have vaiations in the ways they open and close, announce the mysteries and meditations, etc. With that said, the hope is that the formatting of the prayer on this website is both easy and spiritually rich for people new to the prayer to follow along.
